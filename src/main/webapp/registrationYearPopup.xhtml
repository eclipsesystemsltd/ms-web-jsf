Registration Year Calculator

Between the years 1962 and 1983 the last letter of the registration number indicated the year of registration. Registration numbers consisted of three letters followed by two or three numbers and a letter, e.g. ABC 123F.

The registration year corresponds approximately to Triumph's model year but because motorcycles could be registered well after manufacture the registration number is only a guide to the year of manufacture.
